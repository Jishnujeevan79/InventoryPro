About InventoryPro

Smart inventory control built for growing businesses.

InventoryPro is a business-focused inventory management system created to simplify stock tracking, product organization, order flow, and operational visibility in one clean dashboard. It helps teams reduce stock errors, monitor movement faster, and make better day-to-day decisions.

What this system does

The platform is designed to centralize inventory operations for businesses that need a fast, visual, and easy-to-use workspace. It supports stock visibility, product listing, order monitoring, and a better overview of business activity without forcing users through a complex enterprise interface.

Dashboard Insights
Quick access to stock alerts, business metrics, and operational summaries.
Product Control
Manage product catalog, categories, stock levels, and availability clearly.
Order Tracking
Follow order status from pending to delivered with a streamlined layout.

How it helps

Reduces manual confusion

Teams can quickly see what is in stock, what is low, and what needs attention.

Improves visibility

Managers get a cleaner view of inventory health and order movement from one place.

Supports better decisions

Data blocks and visual sections make day-to-day operations easier to understand.

Project identity

This interface was created as a hackathon project with a modern business dashboard approach. The design language is aligned with the dashboard, products, and orders pages so the whole system feels like one connected application rather than separate screens.
